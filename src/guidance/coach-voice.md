# Coach-Voice Style Guide

## DO

- Calm, confident, human tone
- Use words like: "Notice", "start", "choose", "follow-through"
- Permission-giving language
- Short, clear sentences
- Focus on relationships and attention, not tasks

## DON'T

- No "CRM", "pipeline", "lead", "conversion", "automation"
- No "Step 1 / Step 2" numbering
- No guilt or urgency pressure
- No technical jargon
- No overwhelming lists

## Limits

- Title: ≤ 52 characters
- Body: ≤ 240 characters
- Max 3 bullets per step
- Keep it conversational, not instructional

## Examples

**Good:**
- "Welcome back. This is your relationship workspace."
- "Notice what stands out."
- "Start where the energy feels easiest."

**Bad:**
- "Welcome to your CRM dashboard. Step 1: Import your leads."
- "You must complete all tasks to maximize conversion rates."
- "Automate your pipeline to increase efficiency."
